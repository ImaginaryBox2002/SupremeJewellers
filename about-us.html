About Us



For the past three generations, the Midas family has been producing fine designer jewelry. A family tradition that can be traced back to our South African origins.


Even in this day and age of mass production, each of our designs reflects the individual flair and creativity of the craftsman. We see this as an essential part of our production process and our responsibility to you, our clients.


SUPREME JEWELLERS first opened its doors in September 1969, under the direction of Mr. J Midas. The first location was a retail store on Peacock Street, Lenasia , with manufacturing facilities directly above.


Mr J Midas in the 1970s


Mr. J Midas's oldest son, Max Midas, served his traditional apprenticeship in jewelry design and manufacture abroad in 1975 before returning to join the family business and propel it into the modern era.


Mr Max Midas in the 1980s


SUPREME JEWELLERS is now the culmination of many years and generations of tradition. As a member of the community, the brand has expanded its operations in and around Johannesburg, including, among other things, the largest showroom of designer jewelry in Gauteng and our newest showroom, located in Africa's richest square mile, Sandton City.


Whilst the craftsmen at SUPREME JEWELLERS have developed unique and sought after creations for the market, our pieces are manufactured to cater for a wide variety of styles and tastes.


Indeed, we at SUPREME JEWELLERS believe that a commitment to quality and service is a bad idea – as long as it remains an idea. We give it teeth by constantly evaluating our value proposition and actively working to improve it.


SUPREME JEWELLERS Is A Jewellery Manufacturing And Retail Company Known For Its Craftsmanship, Innovation, And Quality. Traditional Jewellery and Bridal Jewellery are among the items in our product line, but are not the only ones.
